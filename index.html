About Me


Alicia Chi is a Chinese American artist based in California. She is currently enrolled at San José State University as a Studio Arts Major,aadwa but looking to pursue the Digital Media Arts Program. She has been creating digital illustrations for the past 2 years before pursuing art as her major in Spring 2022. Her work has since diversified from 2D illustrations into 3D modeling, video, and code.
