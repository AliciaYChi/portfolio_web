Minecraft Building and 3D Modeling


This objective of this peice was to create an object of cultural significance using Minecraft.


The sculpture of Totoro represents the cultural significance of Japanese film, culture, and mythology in overseas media and highlights the increasing acceptance and normalization of Asian culture in mainstream media. It’s a alternative interpretation to the famous 2D icon, as it’s replicated in an open “Sadbox” environment and 3D printed with black filament.


Mediums Used: Minecraft Java edition, Mineways, and 3D printing filament
