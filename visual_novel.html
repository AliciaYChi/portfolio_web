Name:Hoa’s Mundane Daily Adventure


Medium: Bootstrap, CSS, Java Script, and HTML


Dimension:1400 x 800


Description:

Hoa’s Mundane Daily Adventure follows the main character Hoa, as she goes about her time in a new and unfamiliar environment with anxiety and uncertainty. She’s lost and doesn’t have a grasp of herself or her identity, and you as the player follow her on the journey of the day. This story is an abstraction of how I’ve viewed and experienced the world. It’s a story loosely based on myself.
